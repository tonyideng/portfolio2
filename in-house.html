During my time as a Marketing and Graphic Design intern, I was responsible for the creation of various graphic assets.  Working under company brand guidelines was a great creative exercise, I had to take into consideration the market demographics as well as the product image. Some of my most recent work shown here range from web banners, social media assets, ad campaigns, and printed advertisements.

One of my notable contributions was the implementation of a slide show feature on the GeoWarehouse home page. This saved the company time and money because they did not have to contact an outside firm. The art work encapsulates GeoWarehouse's brand and image, and is the result of a team effort between the marketing team, product managers, developers, and myself.
