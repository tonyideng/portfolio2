During my role as the Marketing and Graphic Design intern at Teranet Inc, I assisted the design and development of the ViMO Virtual Mobile Office app. It is a premier sales and marketing support tool for those in the real estate industry, primarily real estate agents. It gives them the ease of mobile technology to provide a better client experience.

Working as a designer alongside a team of developers and the product manager was an eye opening experience. Especially in seeing our designs start out as ideas, then fleshed out in conceptual wire frames, and finally transformed in to fluid application elements. Most of my contributions went into the design of the neighbourhood and augmented reality research functions.
