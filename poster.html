These posters are a set of concept advertisements I created for CanadaHelps.org. The theme is "Brighter", this design is meant to draw focus to the light in the child's eyes and the isolated colours on her clothing/jewelry. This conveys the message that each donation to charities can slowly light up someone's world and make it a little better, or "brighter".
