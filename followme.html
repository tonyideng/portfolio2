I noticed that a lot of people nowadays are active on more than one social media app, especially celebrities and well known organizations. You always hear "follow me on facebook, twitter, instagram etc." I came up with an app concept in which a user can follow their favorite person's social media activity all in one place.

Home Screen

Social Stream

The Social Stream is my concept of an aggregated news feed from all the social media platforms the person that is being followed is on. As you can probably tell I'm a fan of Mr.Bryant, and have synced his Facebook, Twitter, Vine, and Instagram to FollowMe so I can be updated on all of his social media activities. The user can also search for any new apps that Kobe might be on, and sync them to FollowMe.

Social Filtering

Only feel like seeing a few apps' news? The user can filter them to her content with the social filtering function.

FollowMe is still in very conceptual stages, and I hope in the near future when I've become more adept in both design and prorgramming I will be able to turn it into reality! (hopefully with help from my computer science friends on the programming part).
