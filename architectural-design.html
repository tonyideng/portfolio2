This was a  group design project in the DOW Chemical "Design to Zero" competition.

Our design approach was dictated by a unique program of space delegation. Due to the site's space constraints, we calculated the average space a person would need for certain rooms and functions into squares with different areas. We then used this system to layout the space programming and overall design of the 3 structures.

Plan and Structure drawing

This was my final semester design project in architecture in my 3rd year. I was assigned to design a recreational center around the themes of “island” and “isolation”. The building design is multi-purpose, and since I was restricted by the themes, I decided to stack the building programs with a pattern of open and closed spaces.
